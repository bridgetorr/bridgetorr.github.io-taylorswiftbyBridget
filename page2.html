Fearless (Taylor's Version)

Swift’s sophomore album is what really put her on the map. She stuck to her country roots but began to explore the pop verse with some of her biggest hits to date: “Love Story” and “You Belong With Me”. Sonically and lyrically, Fearless pushes naive and hopelessly romantic motifs. Nevertheless, these daydreams might have been the reason they resonated with so many young people around the globe. Additionally, Fearless was the first album Swift re-recorded on her quest to reclaim her music for herself after it was bought out from under her. More on that in Recent News. Finally, Fearless won Swift her first Album of the Year at the Grammys.
