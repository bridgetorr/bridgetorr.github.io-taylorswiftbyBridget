Taylor Swift's Music

Review By Bridget

Taylor Swift is a successful business woman, songwriter, performer, singer, and most recently, director. She has been in the music industry for nearly 20 years, getting her first record deal as a young teenager in Nashville. Since then, Swift has explored many different genres of music; however, her poignant songwriting is what resonates with so many fans across the world. Swift has 9 studio albums, the first 6 of which she is rerecording to regain ownership of her work.
